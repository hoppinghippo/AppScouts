STUDENTS

With App Scouts, you will have the opportunity to apply what you’ve learned in the classroom to create your own games and apps.

 FOLLOW YOUR PASSION

App Scouts provides an opportunity for you to pursue your passion for mobile games and apps while in school.

 A MEANINGFUL EDUCATION

Throughout the program, you will encounter situations that will require you to apply what you’ve learned in the classroom, and actively pursue the answers to your questions.

 SHOWCASE YOUR TALENT

App Scouts provides the opportunity for your creations to be played and reviewed by real users.

 FIND YOUR TALENT

Find where your talent shines in the value chain of a mobile game or app even if you are not a coding genius, you may be the next great character designer in the industry.
